Based on the video, here is a breakdown of the website's style for Word of Life Christian Academy.

### **1. Color Scheme**
The site relies on a high-contrast, complementary color palette that conveys professionalism and warmth.

*   **Primary Color: Deep Navy / Slate Blue**
    *   **Where:** Used in the header, footer, section backgrounds (like the "Distinguished Programming" and "Events" sections), and main text headings.
    *   **Effect:** This color communicates trust, stability, authority, and traditional academic values.
*   **Accent Color: Golden Yellow / Mustard**
    *   **Where:** Used prominently for Call-To-Action (CTA) buttons ("Schedule a Tour," "Apply Now"), icons (star ratings), and graphical highlights.
    *   **Effect:** This provides a strong visual pop against the dark blue, guiding the user's eye to important actions. It adds energy and friendliness to the design.
*   **Background Colors: White and Light Gray**
    *   **Where:** Main content areas to ensure high readability.
    *   **Effect:** Creates a clean, modern look that prevents the interface from feeling cluttered.

### **2. Typography**
*   **Font Family:** The website utilizes a modern **Sans-Serif** typeface (likely something similar to Montserrat, Roboto, or Open Sans).
*   **Style:**
    *   **Headings:** Bold and capitalized or sentence-case, making them easy to scan.
    *   **Body Text:** Clean and legible with generous line spacing.
*   **Readability:** Text is almost always white on dark blue backgrounds or dark grey on white backgrounds, ensuring high contrast and accessibility.

### **3. Layout and Structure**
The design follows a modern, block-based "landing page" structure.

*   **Hero Section:** Features a full-width background video/image with overlaid text and dual CTA buttons (one solid, one ghost/outline style).
*   **Sticky Navigation:** The top menu bar remains fixed or easily accessible, organizing content into clear dropdowns (About, Academics, Admissions).
*   **Card Design:** Information is chunked into "cards." You can see this in the "Events" section and the "Academics" overview, where images are paired with text in uniform rectangular containers.
*   **Bento/Grid Layout:** The social media or gallery section near the bottom uses a masonry or grid layout to display Instagram/Facebook photos.

### **4. Imagery and Visuals**
*   **Photography:** The site relies heavily on high-quality, authentic photography of students and teachers. The images focus on diversity, smiling faces, and active learning environments.
*   **Icons:** Simple, flat vector icons are used for statistics (e.g., "9-12 Student Per Class") and accreditation logos.
*   **Video Integration:** The site uses embedded videos for campus tours and background ambiance, making the school feel dynamic and "alive."

### **5. UI (User Interface) Elements**
*   **Buttons:** Rectangular with slightly rounded corners.
    *   *Primary:* Yellow background with dark text.
    *   *Secondary:* Dark blue or transparent background with white outlines.
*   **Forms:** The "Schedule a Tour" form at the bottom is clean, using standard input fields with clear labels, sitting on a clean white background for ease of use.

### **Summary Vibe**
The overall style is **"Corporate Academic."** It balances the friendly, nurturing aspect required for a daycare/elementary school (warm photos, yellow accents) with the seriousness of an accredited educational institution (navy blue, structured grid, sans-serif fonts).
